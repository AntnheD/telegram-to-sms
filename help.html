How to Get Channel ID and Add Our Channel to Your Group

Getting Channel ID

Using the Telegram App

1. Open the Telegram app on your device.
2. Navigate to the channel you want to add to your group.
3. Tap on the channel name to open the channel information.
4. Scroll down to find the Channel ID.
5. Note down the Channel ID for later use.

Using the Telegram Web

1. Open your web browser and go to web.telegram.org.
2. Log in to your Telegram account.
3. Navigate to the channel you want to add to your group.
4. Click on the channel name to open the channel information.
5. Scroll down to find the Channel ID.
6. Note down the Channel ID for later use.

Adding Our Channel to Your Group

To add our channel to your group, please follow these steps:
1. Open your Telegram app or web interface.
2. Search for our channel using the search bar.
3. Once you find our channel, click on the channel name to open it.
4. Click on the "Join" button to join our channel.
5. Go back to your group and mention our channel's username to share posts from our channel to your group.
